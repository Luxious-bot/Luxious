// 10 — FAQ

QUESTIONS ANSWERED.

What makes Luxious different from a regular chatbot? +
Luxious is an autonomous agent — it doesn't just respond to prompts, it plans and executes multi-step missions. You assign a goal, and Luxious breaks it down, uses tools, makes decisions, and delivers a result. No back-and-forth required.
How do I assign a mission to Luxious? +
Simply open the Luxious bot on Telegram, describe your objective in natural language, and Luxious gets to work. You'll receive status updates and the final result delivered directly to your chat.
What kinds of tasks can Luxious handle? +
Research and competitive analysis, content creation, data processing, workflow automation, document generation, and multi-source information synthesis. If it can be broken into steps and executed with tools, Luxious can handle it.
Does Luxious require me to stay online during a mission? +
No. Luxious operates autonomously. Assign the mission, go do something else, and receive the completed output when it's done. That's the whole point — zero hand-holding.
Is my data safe with Luxious? +
Yes. Luxious processes your requests in isolated sessions and does not store personal mission data after completion. All connections are encrypted and we follow strict data handling policies.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime? +
Absolutely. Cancel anytime directly through Telegram. Access continues until the end of your billing period with no extra charges or penalties.
